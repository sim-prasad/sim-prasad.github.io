IDEA 1 - Digitally Pubslihsing Archived Content from old Women's Weekly Magazines on Tumblr

The idea that I chose to present is idea 1, where I was publishing selected content from the archived magazines on tumblr and seeing what other users reposted around my content. As tumblr allows you to control the content you post on your tumblr pages and choose what you show on your page, I wanted to focus on the dashboard where the content isnt controlled, in order to explore whether the other content posted around mine worked together or had no connection.

A majority of the time most of the content around mine, was unrelateable and completely the opposite of what I presented, but it was really exciting when content that was reposted around mine had some sort of relation to what I had posted, whether it was of a similar context, or the photos just looked similar

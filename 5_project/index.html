IDEA 2 - Similar but Different

In this experiment I used the same content and also the same platform but this time I published selected content from the archived magazines on my tumblr page, rather than focusing on the dashboard. I wanted to stick with the idea of creating my own tumblr page and having the control as a user, however, I will got other people around me to repost content that they like. Anything and everything, whatever they liked. This way I let tumblr do its thing but, did it in an uncontrolled manner, different to other usual pages and how people normally use tumblr.
